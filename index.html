A short, focused engagement that takes you from first conversation to a running automation tool.

1. Requirement Analysis

We assess your current workflows, engineering challenges and project requirements to define exactly what needs to be automated.

2. Workflow Mapping

Our engineers identify every manual step, repetitive task and bottleneck inside your existing CAD or CAE platform.

3. Custom Development

We build tailored scripts and automation tools natively inside your platform, tested against real drawings and models from your project.

4. Testing and Validation

Every solution is validated against your engineering standards and checked for accuracy before anything is handed over to your team.

5. Implementation

We deploy the automation directly into your working environment and walk your team through every feature so adoption is seamless from day one.

6. Ongoing Support

We provide continued support, updates and enhancements as your engineering processes grow and evolve over time.
